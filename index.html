About

The BirdHub was created in February of 2025 by HanmingbirdWebDev, an branch of Hanmingbird Coperated. Hanmingbird Corp.'s other branches include HanmingbirdGames, a game developing company, and BirdStore, a retail store in cooperation with product design company Bear Associates. The BirdHub is designed to combat popular device security systems, such as Securly and GatLabs. This way, students can enjoy gaming time in school; without the restrictions that these common security systems impose.
